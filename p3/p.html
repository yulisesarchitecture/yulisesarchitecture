OLIVIER CACOUB ARCHITECTURAL CENTER, LA GOULETTE, TUNIS

At La Goulette, Tunis, stands a striking new architectural center and museum, a playful assemblage of cubes varying in size and hue, embodying the idea that simplicity can birth complexity and intrigue. This bold statement in geometry, with its sloping roofline, ingeniously ensures that each corner of the building is graced with sweeping views of the gulf of Tunisia, marrying form with the picturesque Mediterranean horizon. It's a place where rigid shapes collide to build dynamism to inspire the visitors and interduce them to the world of architectur.
