ALAYA FASHION CENTER, LAC 0, TUNIS

In the contemporary district of Lac 0, Tunis, a fashion center rises with its captivating exterior, an homage to the fluidity of fabric on the runway. The building's envelope mimics the elegant folds of haute couture garments, creating a dynamic interplay of shadow and light, allowing sunlight to filter through in a soft, evenly distributed ballet of illumination. This architectural marvel not only reflects the textures and layers of fashion but also serves as an iconic beacon for the textile art within the heart of Tunisia.
