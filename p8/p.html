RESTAURATION OF QUASR AMRA, JORDAN

This project aimed at the meticulous reconstruction and 3D modeling of Qasr Amra, a historic gem in Jordan, brings this UNESCO World Heritage site into the digital age. Through advanced 3D technology, every intricate detail of the desert castle's frescoes, architecture, and unique art has been carefully preserved and analyzed. This digital resurrection not only facilitates a deeper understanding of the Islamic architectural marvel but also enhances its accessibility for global audiences, scholars, and preservationists.
