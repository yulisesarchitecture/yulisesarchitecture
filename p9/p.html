ANALYSE OF THE AMAZIGH VILLAGES IN TUNISIA

In my architectural analysis of Tunisian Amazigh villages like Matmata, Taoujout, Tmezret, Toujret, and Zriba, I delve into the harmony between nature and human genuineness. I explore how these communities ingeniously adapt to their harsh environments through troglodyte dwellings and the strategic use of local materials, demonstrating a profound respect for sustainability and cultural identity. My work illuminates the architectural wisdom embedded in these age-old settlements, showcasing a living model of resilience and environmental synergy.
