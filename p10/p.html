KAHINA OLIVE OIL PRESS, TAOUJOUT

In Taoujout, an Amazigh village in the heart of Tunisian desert, a traditional olive oil press has been thoughtfully integrated with habitation units for tourists, offering a unique blend of tradition and hospitality. Part of this remarkable structure is semi-troglodyte, harmoniously embedding itself into the hillside as if climbing the terrain. Its volumes, designed with sensitivity to the landscape, with its bear ground colors that merge seamlessly with the natural environment. This architectural endeavor not only pays homage to the age-old olive oil pressing tradition in the region but also invites guests to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of local culture in a serene landscape.
