FARABI LIBRARY, MEDINA, TUNIS

Nestled within the historic heart of Tunis's Medina, my latest project reimagines the traditional library in a contemporary suit. Whitewashed plaster walls encase a serene courtyard, echoing the intimate patios of local dwellings, while vegetalized terraces bloom above. A geometric dance of cubes informs every design element, beckoning passersby with patterns that promise discovery. This library stands as a curiosity-sparking sanctuary, inviting exploration of knowledge amidst the echoes of the past.
