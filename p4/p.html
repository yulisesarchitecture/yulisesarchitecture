BAYYA GUEST HOUSE, DJERBA

In the heart of Djerba, amidst a whispering palm oasis, lies a guest house that seems to leap from the pages of "One Thousand and One Nights." Its garden, a serene paradise, evokes the enchantment of an ancient oasis, while the building's white surfaces and smooth edges offer a sanctuary of peace. Encircled by the vibrant hues of bougainvillea, the stately elegance of palms, and an ancient olive tree at its core, this retreat is a celebration of Southern Mediterranean tranquility, inviting guests to a world where time stands still.
